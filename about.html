Our History

FROM HUMBLE
BEGINNINGS

Soccer Stars Football Academy was founded in 2015 by former Uganda Cranes midfielder Coach Emmanuel Ssemwogerere and a group of passionate football educators. Starting with just 12 players on a single rented pitch in Lugogo, Kampala, the vision was clear: create a world-class football development pathway right here in Uganda.

By 2017, enrolment had grown to over 150 players, and the academy received its first major sponsorship, enabling the construction of our dedicated training complex. Today, Soccer Stars is home to over 500 active players across all age groups, with alumni playing in Uganda's top leagues and representing the national team at junior levels.

Mission & Vision

OUR PURPOSE &
DIRECTION

🎯

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and develop football talent in Uganda by providing world-class coaching, modern facilities, and a holistic development environment that prepares young players for professional football and life beyond the pitch.

"Every great player was once a beginner. We exist to close that gap."
🔭

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading football academy by 2030 — recognised for producing professional players, model citizens, and future leaders who carry the spirit of Soccer Stars throughout their careers and communities.

"We see a Uganda where homegrown talent dominates the continent."
