Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need prior experience to join?
No prior experience is needed for our Beginner and Intermediate programmes. We welcome everyone who has a passion for football. Our coaches will assess and guide you to the right programme level.
Can players combine programmes?
Yes! Outfield players in any programme can additionally enrol in Goalkeeper Training at a discounted combined rate. Contact us for package pricing.
What equipment do I need?
Academy kit (jersey, shorts, socks) is provided upon enrolment. Players need their own football boots, shin guards, and a water bottle. Balls are provided for all sessions.
Are there scholarship opportunities?
Yes. Elite programme players showing exceptional talent and commitment are considered for our annual scholarship programme, which can cover 50–100% of fees. Speak to our admissions team.
